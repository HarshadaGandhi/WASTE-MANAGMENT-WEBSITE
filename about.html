Goal


To serve as a resource for citizens and businesses to learn about sustainable waste management practices that reduce 
waste generation, promote recycling and composting, and properly dispose of hazardous waste.


Mission


The website aims to empower individuals and communities by providing accessible information and resources on the various 
aspects of waste management, including waste reduction, reuse, recycling, composting, and hazardous waste disposal.


Vision


The website aims to promote a culture of sustainability in which individuals and businesses take responsibility for their waste 
and adopt practices that reduce waste generation, promote recycling and composting, and properly dispose of hazardous waste.
